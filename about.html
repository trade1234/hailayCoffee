More About Hailay Coffee.

Welcome to Hailay Coffee, your premier source for raw coffee beans, directly from the heart of Ethiopia. Founded by Mr. Hailay, our company is dedicated to exporting the finest unroasted coffee, preserving the rich heritage and authentic flavors of Ethiopian coffee.

With a Certificate of Competence for Coffee Export from the Ethiopian Coffee and Tea Authority, Hailay Coffee stands as a testament to quality, sustainability, and authenticity. Our commitment to excellence begins with our meticulous selection of coffee beans, sourced from the lush, fertile regions of Ethiopia renowned for producing some of the world's best coffee.

Mission and Vission.

Our Vision.

At Hailay Coffee, we aim to bridge the gap between Ethiopian coffee farmers and coffee enthusiasts around the globe. We believe in promoting sustainable farming practices and ensuring that the unique flavors of Ethiopian coffee reach every corner of the world.

Our Mission.

Our mission at Hailay Coffee is to provide the highest quality raw coffee beans while fostering sustainable and ethical practices. We strive to support our local farmers, protect the environment, and deliver unparalleled coffee experiences to our customers worldwide. Through dedication and innovation, we aim to elevate the global appreciation of Ethiopian coffee.

Our Values.

Integrity

We uphold integrity by sourcing ethically, ensuring quality, honoring contracts, and fostering fair relationships with partners and stakeholders.

Sustainability

Our company prioritizes sustainability, ensuring our coffee exports promote environmental stewardship, fair trade, and community empowerment for lasting impact.

Reliability

Reliability is our cornerstone, guaranteeing consistent quality, on-time deliveries, and trustworthy partnerships in the global coffee export market.
